Company History

Mangoor A name came from a place in Goa,the place founder born. Which Was founded in th year 2017 by the Founder who dedicates its growth to the place he born.

Mangoor Started with Digital marketing of small shops of Goa and Hyderabad and in 2018 moved forward into eCommerce business A branch of Mangoor named MangoorCart a shopping website basically for Furnitures.
